Where Do They Come From?

Leopard geckos are native to the deserts of **Afghanistan, Pakistan, India, and Iran**. They have been popular pets for decades due to their calm nature and ease of care.

Why Are They Called Leopard Geckos?

Their name comes from their **spotted, leopard-like pattern**. In the wild, this pattern helps them camouflage from predators.

More Facts

Unlike most geckos, leopard geckos have **eyelids** and cannot climb walls!
